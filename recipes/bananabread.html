Banana Bread

Description

This light and fluffy recipe will have you coming back for seconds!

Those overripe bananas that were left on the counter will serve you well in this dish.

It is a delicious treat with the perfect balance of flavors that you and your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups of mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, and lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
